About Us

Welcome to Pit Shop F1 Cafe, located in Sengottai, Tamil Nadu. We've created a unique space where Formula 1 enthusiasts and coffee lovers come together.

Our cafe brings the speed and adrenaline of F1 racing into a cozy environment. Whether you're a hardcore F1 fan or simply looking for exceptional coffee, Pit Shop F1 Cafe is your destination.
